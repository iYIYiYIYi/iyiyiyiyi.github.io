About Me

What’s up you guys !

Welcome to my site. When you saw this page, you might be quite curious about why i want to build this site.

I met a computer when I was 4 years old, and I fell in love with that huge and cumbersome machine on my first eye.

what a miracle machine ! That machine carries the memory of me and my father playing video games together through my whole childhood.

When I was twelve, I received my first tablet, a Lenovo Idea pad s2007, which is almost the only model running Android 3.2 . That was my first time to Android. Using this tablet I’ve learned a lot about computer science. What is the difference between RAM and ROM ? Why that matters of a extra CPU core. And the most important , how to program by using Visual Basic .

I still remember how to execute a script written by Basic on Android, it requires an App which called runtime. I’ve forgotten the name of that developer who built that Basic runtime for Android. Then I practised some tricky scripts on my class teacher’s computer, yes, the teacher in charge of our class.

In my 14s I received my first Android smartphone, Google was still running their business in HongKong. Thus I registered my first gmail, and had the chance to see what Google Play is like before Google quit China entirely.

In the same year I got my another tablet, Android platform still. But you have to say that Google realy did a lot on Android 4.4 , art vm provides a more fluent experience when running applications or playing games. Nvidia Tegra K1 also powered a lot of games with amazing graphics.

Several years later, I studied Java by myself in my spare time.

In the year of 2018, I came into Beijing University of Chemical and Technology for my college life. Majoring Computer Science there, my knowledge of computer grew rapodly. And I rented my first cloud server while learning how to develop web applications.

After formed a total knowledge of web, I started learning game development, from Unity to Unreal, from OpenGL to Vulkan.

Then I decided to get a Master’s degree. Unfortunately, this time I ran out of my good luck and failed in that exam.

I might take that exam again I guess. A better degree allows me to learn more about the admirable electronic machine. In Chine, people call it “electronic brain”.

Now I may be struggle in getting my papers done, or I may already start my career life to make money. But if you want, here it is: yihuang728@gmail.com .

Having a nice day !
